Taj Mahal

Standing gracefully on the banks of the Yamuna River in Agra, the Taj Mahal is one of the most breathtaking monuments ever created. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it represents eternal love and architectural perfection.

History

Construction began in 1632 and involved thousands of artisans, architects, and craftsmen. The monument took over 20 years to complete and used white marble from Rajasthan.
